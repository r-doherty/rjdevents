Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Ryan Doherty

An Events Manager, based in Austin, TX & Kansas City, MO who originally got into the Industry unabashedly thinking I would be able to get free tickets to every show in town. I was incorrect — but am still enjoying the ride. To date, I have learned from and worked with top-flight groups and events including:

  • The PGA Tour
  • The Kansas City Chiefs
  • South By Southwest (SXSW)
  • Austin City Limits Music Festival
  • AEG Presents (Midwest)
  • The Web.com Tour
  • Dancefestopia Music Festival
  • & many others...

To any team I work with — I bring a distinct sense of humor, a deep uncertainty whether personal bios should be written in the first or third-person, and three-plus years experience in fields such as Talent Buying & Contracting, Event Logistics & Operations, Marketing & Analytics, Content Creation, Concert & Festival Production, Accounts Receivable / Accounts Payable (AR/AP), and Ticketing Platform Management.
